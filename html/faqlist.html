Question: Do you rent by the week?
Answer: Yes, we do. Please call for our weekly rates. South Shore Beach Resort is open year-round.






Question: How early can we check in?
Answer: Check in is at 3 p.m. You may check in your luggage prior to 3 p.m. if necessary. The office closes promptly at midnight and no check ins are processed after midnight.






Question: When is check out?
Answer: Check out is at 11 a.m. You may check your luggage after check out if necessary.






Question: Are bachelor or bachelorette parties allowed?
Answer: No bachelor or bachelorette parties are allowed. Unregistered guests may be evicted at the discretion of management.






Question: Do you require a deposit?
Answer: Yes, we require a deposit equivalent to one night's stay within two weeks of booking the reservation. The deposit is applied on the balance due upon arrival. Full payment is due at check in.






Question: Do you take credit cards?
Answer: Yes. We accept Visa, MasterCard and Discover.






Question: Is there someone we can contact once we arrive if there is a problem?
Answer: We maintain an office on premises.
